Privacy Policy (Summary)

Effective date: 15 March 2025

QorTruth is a registered business name of Altura Systems LLC, incorporated in Tbilisi, Georgia. This summary outlines how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard personal information across our websites, newsletters, briefings, and related services (“Services”) under the Law of Georgia on Personal Data Protection and other applicable Georgian legislation.

1. Scope

  • Accessing qortruth.news or any site that links to this policy;
  • Subscribing to newsletters, briefings, alerts, or events we provide;
  • Communicating with our newsroom, editors, or analysts; or
  • Engaging with QorTruth content distributed through partner platforms.

2. Information We Collect

  • Provided by you: newsletter details, correspondence, event registration.
  • Collected automatically: IP, browser/device data, usage analytics, cookies.
  • From partners/public sources: analytics providers, professional directories, and regulatory filings that inform editorial coverage.

3. How We Use Information

  • Deliver requested newsletters, briefings, alerts, and research notes;
  • Tailor content and recommendations to your interests;
  • Protect infrastructure, investigate misuse, and prevent fraud;
  • Analyze engagement to improve editorial coverage and product experience;
  • Manage events, community programs, and stakeholder outreach;
  • Comply with legal obligations and enforce our Terms of Service.

4. Legal Bases

  • Consent for newsletters, marketing, and non-essential cookies;
  • Contract performance to deliver requested Services;
  • Legitimate interests to operate, secure, and improve QorTruth;
  • Legal obligations under Georgian law, including the Law of Georgia on Personal Data Protection and guidance from the Personal Data Protection Service of Georgia.

5. Sharing

  • Service providers supporting hosting, analytics, communications, security, and support;
  • Business transfers in the event of a merger, acquisition, restructuring, or asset sale;
  • Legal compliance to meet obligations or protect rights and users;
  • With consent when you authorize collaboration with third parties.

6. International Transfers

QorTruth operates from Georgia. If you access the Services from outside Georgia, your information may be processed in Georgia and other jurisdictions where our service providers operate. We use contractual safeguards, including standard contractual clauses and data processing agreements, to protect your information.

7. Retention & Security

Data is retained only as long as necessary for stated purposes or legal obligations. Security measures include access controls, encryption in transit where suitable, vendor due diligence, and staff training, but no system is entirely secure—protect your credentials and notify us of suspected misuse.

8. Your Choices & Rights

  • Update or opt out of newsletters via unsubscribe links or by contacting us.
  • Manage non-essential cookies through browser settings or consent tools.
  • Request access, correction, deletion, restriction, or portability of your data, or withdraw consent where applicable.
  • Residents of Georgia may file complaints with the Personal Data Protection Service of Georgia.

9. Children

The Services are intended for users aged 16 and older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 16 without verified parental consent.

10. Updates & Contact

We may revise this policy to reflect operational, legal, or regulatory changes and will update the effective date accordingly. For questions or to exercise your rights, contact Altura Systems: privacy@qortruth.news or Privacy Office, 12 Rustaveli Ave, Tbilisi 0108, Georgia.
